Roadmap

What's been shipped,
what's coming next.

A small, independent project — so the roadmap is honest and short. Last updated: 20 May 2026.

✓ Shipped already in the app

  • Local subscription trackingAdd, edit, delete, pause subs. Categories, tags, ratings, notes. Multi-currency with daily ECB rates.
  • Smart bank-CSV importRevolut, Wise, N26, PayPal, Chase, Amex — column headers detected in 28 languages.
  • Renewal & trial notificationsLocal browser notifications 1/3/7 days before. Trial-end warnings.
  • Spending insights & trendsMonthly/yearly totals, category donut, 6-month trend line, lifetime cost, "if invested instead" math.
  • Cloud Sync (Pro)End-to-end encrypted, AES-GCM 256, PBKDF2 600 000. Stored in your own Google Drive.
  • Email Reminders (Pro)Backup channel for renewal alerts via Cloudflare Worker → Brevo.
  • Business Tax Report (Pro)PDF + CSV of every business-flagged subscription, ready for your accountant.
  • 28 languages, dark/light modes, PWA installFull-localized UI, A11y improvements (focus trap, ARIA labels), offline-first.
  • Goals, budgets, streaksYearly savings goal, monthly category budgets, "clean days" streak counter.
  • Privacy & legal foundationGDPR-compliant privacy policy in 2 langs, T&C with EU consumer rights, ÁSZF acceptance recorded with timestamp + version, DPIA for Cloud Sync.
  • One-click Drive blob deletion when sync is turned offDisabling Cloud Sync now offers to permanently delete the encrypted backup file from your Google Drive in the same step (GDPR Art. 17 "right to be forgotten" — closed in app).
  • Chrome extension at feature parityThe browser-extension build now ships the same Drive-delete flow and all 28 locale bundles as the PWA. Same sync.js, same disable flow, same translations — installed as a popup action from the Chrome toolbar.

⟳ In progress shipping this quarter

  • Master-password strength meterReal-time entropy hint when setting up Cloud Sync. Helps users pick something better than "password1".Q3 2026
  • Play Store TWA releaseTrusted Web Activity wrapper, native Android purchase via Google Play Billing.Q3 2026

→ Next on the list

  • Spending alerts beyond budget"You're trending 12% higher than last quarter" — proactive nudges based on the trend chart.
  • Multi-account profilesSwitch between "personal" and "company" lists without two separate browsers.
  • iOS Safari "Add to Home Screen" polishiOS PWAs have quirks — push notifications via APNs require Safari 16.4+, sync via Apple ID requires more work.
  • Bank format supportAdding more European banks (OTP, Erste, ING, BBVA, Santander, BNP Paribas).
  • "Cancel link" expansionCurrently ~60 services have a built-in cancel URL. Goal: 200+.

? Considering if enough users ask

  • Web-extension companionDetect "Are you sure you want to subscribe?" moments in the browser and offer a one-click add-to-leakd.
  • Bulk SMS / WhatsApp remindersFor users who want renewal alerts outside email and push.
  • Family plan UIThe data model already supports `sharedWith`. A proper family-pricing view could go further.
  • Self-hosted Pro SyncFor users who don't want Google Drive — point sync at their own WebDAV server.

✗ Not planned on purpose

  • Bank integration (Plaid / Tink / GoCardless)Invasive, geographically limited, requires us to be a regulated entity. Not happening.
  • Analytics & behaviour trackingThe whole point of leakd is that we don't watch you.
  • "Enterprise" / "Teams" pricingThis is a personal-finance tool. Pricing is $0 or $5/mo. No tiers.
  • AI chat / "Ask leakd anything"The math is simple enough that you don't need a chatbot. LLM costs would force us to track usage. Not worth it.
  • Cryptocurrency supportOut of scope. Use a dedicated crypto-tracker if you need it.
